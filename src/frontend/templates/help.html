THE GRAPH
You can scroll up to zoom in and down to zoom out, however due to the size of the data it may be quite glitchy. Instead, consider drawing a box around the given data to zoom.

Double click anywhere on the chart to zoom back out.


THE SENTIMENT
Scores range from 0-10, but due to the number of comments considered calculated scores tend to skew more towards 5.

Thus, scores less than 4 indicate significant negative opinion, while scores greater than 6 indicate notable positive standings.


THE SEARCHBAR
For the best results, enter the company name or stock ticker precisely. As an easier option, click on a suggested autocomplete option.

A section to clear up some less intuitive features.
